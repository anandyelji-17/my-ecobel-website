About ECOBEL

ECOBEL is a bamboo tissue paper company established in December 2025 by Anand Yelji and Saish Shindolkar. We focus on creating sustainable alternatives to conventional tissue paper with the aim to reduce deforestation and single-use waste.

Our Story

Founded out of a desire to offer practical, sustainable alternatives to everyday products, ECOBEL started with a simple idea: if we change the material we use for disposable products, we can scale positive environmental impact rapidly. Mission: Replace regular tissues with bamboo tissue papers through quality manufacturing and transparent sourcing.
