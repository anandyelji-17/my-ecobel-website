About ECOBEL

ECOBEL is a bamboo tissue paper company established in December 2025 by Anand Yelji and Saish Shindolkar. Our mission is to replace regular tissues with bamboo tissue papers — reducing deforestation and single-use waste.

Our Story

Started from a desire to combine product quality with environmental responsibility, ECOBEL manufactures bamboo-based tissues and alternatives designed for both consumers and businesses.

Vision & Mission

Vision: A world where everyday essentials are sustainable.
Mission: Replace regular tissues with bamboo tissue papers through quality manufacturing and transparent sourcing.
